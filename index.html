Our Work in Action

About Us

Sadaqah Jariyah Welfare Center is established with the sincere intention of serving humanity for the pleasure of Allah (SWT). We believe that true charity is not limited to one-time help, but is a continuous effort that benefits people for years to come.

Our welfare center focuses on long-term, sustainable solutions such as education, healthcare, food security, clean living conditions, and community development. Every project we undertake is planned carefully so that the reward continues even after the donor and volunteer have passed away — this is the essence of Sadaqah Jariyah.

With the help of kind-hearted donors, volunteers, and supporters, we aim to build a society where no one is left hungry, uneducated, or without basic medical care.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide continuous charity through welfare activities that improve lives, strengthen communities, and bring long-term positive change, while maintaining transparency, dignity, and compassion in all our actions.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate society where every individual has access to education, healthcare, food, and basic necessities, and where charity becomes a lifelong investment in the hereafter.

Our Causes & Projects

We focus on welfare projects that create long-lasting benefits for individuals and communities. Each initiative is designed to uplift lives with dignity and compassion.

Education Support

Providing access to quality education for underprivileged children through school fees, books, uniforms, scholarships, and Islamic education programs.

Food & Nutrition

Regular food distribution, ration kits, and emergency relief for families facing hunger, ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

Medical Assistance

Supporting medical treatments, medicines, health camps, and emergency care for the poor and needy.

Orphan & Widow Care

Providing financial, emotional, and educational support to orphans and widows so they can live with dignity and hope.

Water & Sanitation

Installation of water wells, drinking water facilities, and sanitation projects to improve health and hygiene in needy areas.

Sadaqah Jariyah Projects

Long-term charity initiatives such as building classrooms, clinics, water wells, and community centers where rewards continue for generations.

Qur’an & Hadith on Charity

Islam strongly encourages charity and caring for those in need. Giving for the sake of Allah purifies wealth, softens hearts, and brings immense reward in this world and the Hereafter.

Qur’an

“The example of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a seed which grows seven spikes; in each spike is a hundred grains. And Allah multiplies for whom He wills.” (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:261)

Hadith

The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said: “Charity does not decrease wealth, and Allah increases the honor of the one who forgives.” (Sahih Muslim)
